Does the lack of Accessibility design in web development impact businesses negatively?
As part of my university dissertation, I worked on a project that responded to that question and provided an example of an accessible E-commerce website.

This project works with MongoDB, jQuery, Paypal API and Sass. A variety of tools were used in order to measure it's accessibility, most of which are online audits of the site of which passed at a 95% rate. Mobile Friendly.
